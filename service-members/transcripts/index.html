You can request a transcript or score report be sent to your college of choice.


When requesting a transcript or grade report, be very specific as to where it is to be sent. Include any office symbol, code or particular person at the institution that is to receive the transcript (i.e., admissions, evaluations, graduation, etc.)


  • Requesting Transcripts Before July 1, 1974

    United States Armed Forces Institute (USAFI):
    GED, General Examinations of the College Level and USAFI Courses

    Between 1942 and 1974, the United States Armed Forces Institute (USAFI) provided education opportunities to Service members in the U.S., Alaska, Caribbean, Europe, Hawaii and Japan.

    Please use the USAFI/GED Transcript Order Form.

    Go to Website

  • Requesting Transcripts After July 1, 1974

    Transcripts and Grade Reports for Exams Sponsored by DANTES

    Service members seeking transcripts for DANTES-funded or self-funded exams taken after 1 July 1974 may download a transcript order form by visiting the respective testing agency.


    • CLEP and DSST Go to Website
    • Self-Funded CLEP Retests Go to Website
    • ACT Go to Website
    • SAT Go to Website
    • GMAT Go to Website
    • GRE Go to Website
    • PRAXIS Go to Website
    • ECE (not funded by DANTES effective 1 Oct 11) Go to Website
    • LSAT (not funded by DANTES) Go to Website


  • GED Taken by Service Members and Affiliated Civilians at U.S. and Overseas Military Installations

    Please visit GED Testing Service's Request a Transcript page.

    • Select DANTES for the Jurisdiction

    • Click Create Account for access to the online order system

    • Use the account to request transcripts online and track status of orders



    Go to Website


    Direct questions about transcripts to GED Testing Service via email: communication@gedtestingservice.com or call 1-877-EXAM-GED (877-392-6433).
