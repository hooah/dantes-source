3rd Party Assessment Program

The purpose of the 3rd Party Assessment is to assess the quality of off-duty post-secondary educational programs and services used by service members regardless of location or duty assignment and to assist in the improvement of these education programs and services. This periodic assessment will ensure the education programs provided to service members funded by a tuition assistance program are of the same high quality and meet the same academic criteria of quality higher education.
