What you need to know about Air National Guard Tuition Assistance

Air National Guard Tuition Assistance is a program that provides 100 percent tuition and fees for courses taken by activated Guard and Reserve members.


  • Am I Eligible?

    If activated, you will become eligible for the Air Force Tuition Assistance for Active Duty and Activated Reserve/Guard.

  • What Will I Receive?

    100 percent of tuition and fees, not to exceed:

    • Up to $250 at semester credit hour
    • $166 at quarter credit hour
    • $4,500 at fiscal year

  • What Kind of Programs are Available?

    Your Air National Guard Tuition Assistance can be use to pay for many different education programs, including:

    • Academic or technical degree programs or courses taken from two-or four-year institutions
    • May be taken on base, off base or by correspondence
