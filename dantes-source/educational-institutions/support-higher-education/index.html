Support Higher Education for Service Members


  • Join the Post 9/11 GI Bill Yellow Ribbon and Montgomery GI Bill programs. List all online programs in the DANTES catalog, allowing students to get tuition reimbursement support under DANTES.
  • Recognize CLEP and DANTES exams. Extend scholarships and financial aid to service members.
  • Offer tuition discounts and flexible payment options to service members.
  • DANTES will provide support at every stage of the process as your institution establishes procedures to accommodate service members.
